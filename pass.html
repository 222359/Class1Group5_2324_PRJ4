The Theatralia Passes

How it works

At Theatralia, we want to share the transformative power of theatre with you! The Theatre Pass is a subscription-based ticket to your favorite shows. Whether you love local plays by student organizations, regional plays with your country's favorite actors or the glitz and glam of the international stage, the Theatre Pass has exactly what works for you.

Benelux Pass

The Benelux Pass is the largest package Theatralia has to offer. With access to all the large theatre venues in Belgium, The Netherlands and Luxembourg, you're sure to not miss a single run of your favorite well-known plays! But not to worry, with this pass you can also visit every partnered smaller venue within our reach, so that you can also discover the diamonds in the rough.

Learn more

Country Pass

Are you planning a roadtrip through Belgium, The Netherlands or Luxembourg? Or are you excited to dive deep into your country's performance arts? Then the Country Pass is perfect for you! This pass allows you to visit all the partnered venues and organisations in one country. This allows you to explore the country's cultural performances to its fullest!

Learn more

Local Pass

Are you ready to discover the next big musical star? Or are you curious to see what your local theatre community is up to? Choose the Local Pass! With this pass, you have unlimited access to every performance venue for the performance arts in and around one city. Explore the local gems that EttenLeur in the Netherlands has to offer or see an opera performance in Esch-sur-Alzette in Luxembourg. Magic can be found in every corner of the world, even the smallest ones.

Learn more

Selecting your perfect pass

First, select the region you want to visit. You can keep it simple with the Local Pass. This allows you to select one major city (and a couple surrounding villages), and you can visit any partnered theatre in the city whenever you want. If you're thinking bigger is better, the country pass might work for you. With this pass, you can visit an entire country's worth of participating theatre venues and organizations. And if you're a true theatre fan and can't miss out on anything, you can go for our Benelux pass, allowing you unlimited access to every play, show and venue that Theatralia has partnerships with! Next, you'll select whether you want a monthly or a yearly subscription. If you live in one of the countries we're located in, you'll always have access to great performances just around the corner with our yearly pass. However, if you're visiting a region for a holiday or a trip, take a look at our monthly passes. This way you can explore the arts to your hearts content before you head back home. To find out which pass works best for you, browse through our theatre venues. This is where you can find links to the venue's agenda as well as our the prices per option, so you can get the most magical experience for an even more enchanting price!
