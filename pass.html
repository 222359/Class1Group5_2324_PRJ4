The Theatralia Passes

Benelux Pass

The Benelux Pass is the largest package Theatralia has to offer. With access to all the large theatre venues in Belgium, The Netherlands and Luxembourg, you're sure to not miss a single run of your favorite well-known plays! But not to worry, with this pass you can also visit every partnered smaller venue within our reach, so that you can also discover the diamonds in the rough.

Learn more

Country Pass

Are you planning a roadtrip through Belgium, The Netherlands or Luxembourg? Or are you excited to dive deep into your country's performance arts? Then the Country Pass is perfect for you! This pass allows you to visit all the partnered venues and organisations in one country. This allows you to explore the country's cultural performances to its fullest!

Learn more

Local Pass

Are you ready to discover the next big musical star? Or are you curious to see what your local theatre community is up to? Choose the Local Pass! With this pass, you have unlimited access to every performance venue for the performance arts in and around one city. Explore the local gems that EttenLeur in the Netherlands has to offer or see an opera performance in Esch-sur-Alzette in Luxembourg. Magic can be found in every corner of the world, even the smallest ones.
