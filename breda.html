Breda

A real showstopper in the middle of our founding city, Chassé theatre is a staple for the performing arts in Breda in the south of the Netherlands. The venue is known for their unconventional performances by large and smaller artists. It allows students from local drama schools to perform there, as well as provide national productions with a suitable stage and many more.

Breda

Chassé hosts tours for elementary schools to get acquainted with the arts, workshops for smaller theatre classes and regularly hosts events for third-party organizations, all in their beautiful red velvet hallways. Chassé has 3 theatre halls and receives over 400.000 visitors every year. This unique building is a true local hub for all things arts.
