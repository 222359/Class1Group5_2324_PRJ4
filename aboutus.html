Our vision

Accessibility

We want everyone to be able to visit the theatre, regardless of how much money you have or where you live. In a world where theatre is seen as a luxury, we want to provide you with an option to experience the magic up close without breaking the bank. Theatregoers all over the world struggle with being able to afford to participate in the art, whether that is visiting shows or joining meet-ups. This is why we provide you with one price for our theatre pass. You can use this to visit unlimited shows for one, low monthly price.

Fostering connections

Welcome to the show! Invite your friends and family to the wonderous world of the performing arts. As fellow theatre lovers, we know that theatre is best when you share it with the ones you love and even better when you share it with a whole community! Feel the roar of a standing ovation, speak up in the discussion with the audience and feel the thrill of meeting your favorite performers at the stage door with your very best friends.

Passion for the Performing Arts

We love theatre just as much as you do! We get the goosebumps when the lights dim, we feel the excitement when a new show is announced, and we are just as excited about listening to showtunes on the way as you are. We make sure our prices are fair for both parties so that you can enjoy the transformative power of theatre without a worry/with ease.
