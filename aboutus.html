About Theatralia

Technically, Theatralia was founded in 2024. For the founders, however, it has been a lifelong dream to make theatre and performing arts accessible to everyone. After seeing a recurring problem with limited access to the theatre due to the high prices of tickets in their friends in the theatre community, Diana, Quinten, Noah and Eliza decided to get to work. Together they started a rapidly growing company to make sure a larger audience could see the performance arts. For now, the company is limited to select venues in The Netherlands, Belgium and Luxembourg, but as developments continue, we will expand to include more and more theatre venues and locations all throughout Europe. Together we can spread the transformative power of theatre to all the corners of europe!

Our vision & mission

We want to make sure everyone gets enchanted by the transformative power of theatre. The magic of the stage is meant to be shared by everyone who wants to see it, not just those who can afford the high ticket price that performances often require.

We aim to provide European theatre audiences with an affordable, subscription based pass with which they gain unlimited access to select theatres.

Our values

Accessibility 

In a world where theatre is seen as a luxury, we want to provide you with an option to experience the magic up close without breaking the bank. Theatregoers all over the world struggle with being able to afford to participate in the art, whether that is visiting shows or joining meet-ups. This is why we provide you with one price for our theatre pass.

Connection

Welcome to the show! Invite your friends and family to the wonderous world of the performing arts. As fellow theatre lovers, we know that theatre is best when you share it with the ones you love and even better when you share it with a whole community! Feel the roar of a standing ovation, speak up in the discussion with the audience and feel the thrill of meeting your favorite performers at the stage door with your very best friends

Passion

We get the goosebumps when the lights dim, we feel the excitement when a new show is announced, and we are just as excited about listening to showtunes on the way as you are. The theatre-industry is a complex and expensive one, so we are here to bring the magic to your shows. We make sure our prices are fair for both parties so that you can enjoy the transformative power of theatre without a worry.

Collaboration

The magic of theatre is amplified when we combine cultures into one harmony of arts. We believe that every voice is worth hearing, so we want to include as many different perspectives into our projects as possible. Not only is our own team dedicated to fostering collaborations with each other, we also include local and international artists, community organizations and theatre institutions in our partnerships. This way, we can widen our horizons to truly meet the magic that different cultures and people bring to the table.
